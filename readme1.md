# 🎉 Nekath Seettuwa 2026 – Sinhala & Tamil New Year App

**Nekath Seettuwa 2026** is the ultimate companion for celebrating the traditional **Sinhala and Tamil New Year**. Packed with cultural significance, smart notifications, and a beautiful festive design — this app brings every Aurudu moment to life right at your fingertips.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🗓️ Complete Nekath Schedule (2026)
All **9 essential Aurudu Nekath events** with accurate times and detailed descriptions — sourced from the official Sinhala New Year Nakath Committee.

### ⏳ Live Countdown Timers
Real-time countdown for every upcoming Nekath moment, so you never miss a ritual. Each Nakath has its own dedicated screen with timer, cultural imagery, and description.

### 🔔 Smart Nakath Notifications *(New)*
Automatic push notifications at **15, 10, and 5 minutes** before each Nekath — plus an alert at the **exact moment** it arrives. Never miss a single Nakath again, even when the app is closed.

### 🧭 Built-in Compass
Find the auspicious direction for rituals like **ගනුදෙනු කිරීම** (ganu denu) and **දිග ගත් කළ යුතු දිශාව** with our smooth, easy-to-use digital compass.

### 🎵 Aurudu Songs Collection *(Enhanced)*
A curated library of **32 classic Aurudu songs** — streamed directly from the cloud with a full-featured music player:
- ▶️ Play / Pause / Stop controls
- ⏭️ Next & Previous track navigation
- 🎚️ Seekable progress bar with duration display
- 🎨 Animated "Now Playing" panel with pulse effects
- 🔄 Auto-play next song on completion
- 🌐 Internet connectivity check before streaming

### 🎶 Background Music *(New)*
Immersive ambient Aurudu BGM that plays automatically on app launch and intelligently pauses when you play a song from the collection.

### ⭐ In-App Rating & Feedback *(New)*
Share your thoughts with a modern, Play Store–style rating dialog:
- 5-star rating system with animated interactions
- Optional text feedback (up to 500 characters)
- Submissions stored securely via **Firebase Cloud Firestore**
- Spam protection with 30-second submission cooldown
- Contextual thank-you messages based on rating

### 💬 Live Feedback Carousel *(New)*
See what other users are saying! Recent reviews are displayed in an auto-scrolling carousel — fetched in real-time from Firebase.

### 🎨 Premium Festive UI
A stunning, dark-themed interface with:
- ✨ Gold shimmer gradients & glassmorphism effects
- 🌟 Smooth page transitions & micro-animations
- 🎆 Animated splash screen with loading effects
- 🔤 Custom Sinhala fonts (TharuDigitalNikini, FM Arjun)
- 📱 Fully responsive across all Android screen sizes

### 🛡️ Network Awareness *(New)*
Graceful handling of offline scenarios with friendly "Connection Lost" dialogs — ensuring a smooth experience even without internet.
